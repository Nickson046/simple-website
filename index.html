Fuel Guide

Petrol or Diesel —
Which One Is For You?

Not sure which fuel your car takes? Here's a simple guide. When in doubt, our staff at any NERO station are always happy to help.

⛽
Petrol

Petrol Fuel

Petrol is for regular everyday cars and smaller engines. If you drive a normal car — saloon, hatchback, SUV or minibus — it almost certainly takes petrol.

🚗
Saloon cars, hatchbacks, sedans
🚐
Minibuses and small vans
🏍️
Motorcycles and boda bodas
🚙
Most SUVs and 4x4s
Quick check: Open your fuel cap — it usually says "PETROL" or "UNLEADED". You can also check your car handbook or ask our staff.
🛢
Diesel

Diesel Fuel

Diesel is for bigger, heavier engines — especially trucks, lorries, buses and some large SUVs. Diesel engines are more powerful and economical over long distances.

🚛
Trucks, lorries and heavy vehicles
🚌
Buses and large passenger vehicles
🚜
Farm equipment and generators
🚙
Some large pickup trucks & 4x4s
Warning: Never put petrol in a diesel engine or diesel in a petrol engine. If you're unsure, ask our staff before fuelling — we're always here to help.
🙋
Still Not Sure? Just Ask!

Our staff at every NERO station are trained to help you choose the right fuel. Never guess — a wrong fuel fill can damage your engine. We're always happy to check for you.
